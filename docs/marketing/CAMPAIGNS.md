# 🚀 Campaign Strategy - SalesCRM

## 📋 Overview

Plan estratégico de campañas para generar leads calificados y escalar SalesCRM en el mercado B2B.

---

## 🎯 Campaign Objectives

### **Primary Goals**
- **Generate 100+ MQLs monthly**
- **Achieve $25 CPL or less**
- **Maintain 5%+ conversion rate**
- **Scale to $10,000 MRR in 6 months**

### **Success Metrics**
- **Lead Quality**: >40% demo rate
- **Cost Efficiency**: <$25 per qualified lead
- **Pipeline Value**: >$50,000 monthly
- **ROI**: >300% on ad spend

---

## 🎯 Target Audience Definition

### **Primary ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)**

#### **Company Profile**
- **Size**: 10-100 employees
- **Revenue**: $1M - $20M annually
- **Industry**: SaaS, Services, Education, Consulting
- **Growth Stage**: Series A or bootstrapped, growing

#### **Decision Maker Profile**
- **Role**: CEO, Sales Manager, VP of Sales
- **Age**: 30-45 years old
- **Experience**: 5-15 years in sales/management
- **Pain Points**: Lead leakage, low conversion, manual processes

### **Secondary Targets**
- **Sales Operations Managers**
- **Business Development Managers**
- **Sales Team Leaders (5-20 reps)**
- **Startup Founders with sales teams**

## 🎨 Creative Strategy

### **Ad Copy Framework**

#### **Problem-Agitate-Solve (PAS)**
```
Problem: "¿Perdiendo leads por seguimiento manual?"
Agitation: "Cada lead sin follow-up es $5,000 perdidos"
Solution: "SalesCRM automatiza 80% de tu seguimiento"
CTA: "Prueba gratis 14 días"
```

#### **Before-After-Bridge (BAB)**
```
Before: "Gestionando 50 leads en Excel"
After: "Pipeline automatizado con IA"
Bridge: "SalesCRM conecta WhatsApp, email y CRM"
CTA: "Ver cómo funciona"
```

#### **Feature-Benefit-Proof (FBP)**
```
Feature: "Asistente IA integrado"
Benefit: "Cierra 40% más deals con recomendaciones inteligentes"
Proof: "500+ empresas ya confían en nosotros"
CTA: "Agendar demo personalizada"
```

### **Visual Guidelines**

#### **Brand Colors**
- **Primary**: Blue gradient (#667eea → #764ba2)
- **Secondary**: Clean white (#ffffff)
- **Accent**: Green for success (#10b981)
- **Text**: Dark gray (#1f2937)

#### **Image Style**
- **Professional**: Business settings, office environments
- **Diverse**: Multi-ethnic teams and customers
- **Tech-focused**: Screenshots of the product
- **Authentic**: Real customer photos when possible

#### **Video Specifications**
- **Length**: 15-30 seconds for feed, 60 seconds for stories
- **Format**: 1080x1080 square, 1080x1920 vertical
- **Subtitles**: Spanish subtitles required
- **Branding**: Logo watermark in corner

## 🔄 A/B Testing Framework

### **Testing Priorities**

#### **High Impact Tests**
1. **Headlines**: 6 variants
2. **CTA buttons**: 4 variants
3. **Images**: 8 variants
4. **Ad copy**: 5 variants

#### **Medium Impact Tests**
1. **Landing pages**: 3 variants
2. **Form fields**: Reduction test
3. **Social proof**: Different formats
4. **Pricing presentation**: 2 variants

#### **Low Impact Tests**
1. **Colors**: Button and accent colors
2. **Fonts**: Headline typography
3. **Layout**: Element arrangement
4. **Timing**: Dayparting tests

### **Testing Methodology**

#### **Statistical Significance**
- **Minimum sample size**: 100 conversions per variant
- **Confidence level**: 95%
- **Test duration**: 7-14 days
- **Winner selection**: Highest conversion rate

#### **Multivariate Testing**
- **Elements tested**: Headline + Image + CTA
- **Combinations**: 2x2x2 = 8 variants
- **Traffic split**: Equal distribution
- **Analysis**: Full factorial analysis
